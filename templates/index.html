rdrr

A less convenient URL shortener.



How?

Enter a URL in the first input and a few words or a short sentence in the second input, then hit submit. You will be given a link that will eventually redirect someone to the given URL, after being bounced around a few times on pages displaying the text you entered.

Why?

Because its just the right balance of silly, annoying, and ueslessness.
